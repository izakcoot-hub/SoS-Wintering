Introducing... When Women Return to Rhythm

When Women Return to Rhythm is actually an ongoing series of retreats designed to help high-functioning, perfectionist moms (women who nurture others) reclaim their vitality, purpose, and joy by reconnecting with the natural cycles of their body, mind, and spirit through a powerful blend of somatic practices, nervous system regulation, and sacred feminine wisdom.

More than just a step out of the hustle into a deeply restorative 4 day reset, this experience provides the opportunity to receive continued seasonal guidance regular retreats and 3 months post retreat support, so the change becomes integrated and doesn't end when you leave.

Each seasonally-aligned retreat is shaped around a different natural cycle, gently guiding you through a journey of transformation—from overwhelmed, depleted, and disconnected to deeply rooted, joyful, and aligned with your feminine nature, supported by the unique energy of that cycle.

The Retreat Venue: Bodhi Khaya

Bodhi Khaya is a tranquil sanctuary for retreat and renewal, nestled in the biodiverse landscape of the Walker Bay Conservancy in South Africa's beautiful Overberg region. Set across hundreds of hectares of indigenous fynbos, milkwood forests and mountain trails, it offers a deeply peaceful environment where you can rest, reconnect and be truly present.

Guests are invited to slow down and immerse themselves in nature and the serene surroundings naturally support introspection, restoration, and a sense of homecoming—making it an ideal setting for retreats focused on inner renewal.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How far is the retreat venue from the nearest airport?

The nearest airport is Cape Town International Airport which is a 2h20min drive to Bodhi Khaya. We've carefully chosen a location that balances accessibility with the opportunity to truly disconnect and reconnect with yourself. Rest assured, we'll provide you with all the specific travel details upon confirmation of your enrolment.

Q: Will transport be included in the cost?

No, transport is not included in the cost and will need to be arranged by you. However, if enough participants are coming from the airport we'll help coordinate shared lifts or a shuttle option (at your own cost). This approach not only makes travel more affordable but also creates an opportunity for connection before the retreat even begins. So once you've signed up let us know if you are open to shared transport and let's see what we can arrange.

Q: Can I get a massage while there?

Absolutely! Bodhi Khaya offers a variety of different styles of massage treatments to suit your needs. You will have sufficient time between facilitation sessions to relax and enjoy a spa treatment (at your own cost).

Q: Will I be able to access wifi?

Yes, WiFi is available at Bodhi Khaya, but it is primarily located in the common areas. While we encourage guests to disconnect and immerse themselves in nature, the limited, communal WiFi allows for connectivity if needed. Note that cell phone reception can also be limited.

Q: How many people will be on the retreat?

We've intentionally designed an intimate, supportive container that allows for deep personal transformation while providing a sense of community. Numbers will be limited to 12.

Q: Can I drink, smoke, or use other substances during the retreat?

We invite you to approach this retreat as a sacred space of healing and transformation. While we understand these are personal choices, we strongly recommend staying "clean" during the retreat to support your deepest healing. We ask that you refrain from bringing any alcohol or substances with you and, if you must smoke, please do so outside and away from others. This isn't about restriction — it's about creating the purest, most supportive environment for your personal healing journey.
